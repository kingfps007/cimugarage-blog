# Privacy Policy

> Effective: 2026-07-02
> Applies to: cimugarage.cn (including www and en subdomains)

## 1. What We Collect

| Data | Required | Public |
|------|----------|--------|
| Nickname | Yes (for comments) | Yes |
| Comment content | Yes | Yes |
| Email | Optional | **No** (hashed before storage) |
| Website | Optional | Yes (URL only) |
| View count | Automatic | Yes (aggregate only) |

## 2. What We Don't Collect

- ❌ Real name
- ❌ Phone number
- ❌ Geolocation
- ❌ Browser fingerprint
- ❌ Cross-site tracking ID

## 3. Cookies

This site uses **no tracking cookies**. The only cookie is the **comment draft** (stored locally only, never sent to server), which saves your half-written comment against page refresh.

## 4. Data Storage

All comment data is stored in **Alibaba Cloud ESA Pages KV** (China mainland region). View counts and rankings share the same KV.

## 5. Your Rights (PIPL §44)

Under China's Personal Information Protection Law Article 44, you have:

- **Access**: View all comments you have submitted
- **Correction**: Modify personal info (nickname, email)
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of one or more of your comments
- **Account closure**: Permanent removal of all your data

**Response time**: 7 business days.

### How to Exercise

When you submit a comment, the system returns a **one-time query token** (saved in your browser's localStorage + sent to your email). To query/delete later, email us with the token for verification.

> We **do not** log raw IP addresses—only `sha256(ip + salt)` hashes are stored, and the salt is not public. If you prefer token-less verification (e.g., email-based), please contact the site owner.

## 6. Data Retention

- Comments: 1 year from submission, then periodic cleanup
- View counts: retained permanently (aggregate data only)

## 7. Minors

Comments from users under 14 are not accepted. The site owner will proactively delete any such comments discovered.

## 8. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Major changes are announced on the homepage 7 days before taking effect.
